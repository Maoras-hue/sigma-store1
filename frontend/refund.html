Refund Policy

At Sigma Studio, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
If you're not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a refund within 30 days.

Digital Products Refund Policy

Since our products are digital and delivered instantly, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.

Eligible for Refund:

  • Product doesn't work as described
  • Product contains major defects or bugs
  • You accidentally purchased the wrong product (within 24 hours)
  • Duplicate purchase of the same product
Not Eligible for Refund:
  • Change of mind after download
  • Didn't read product description properly
  • Compatibility issues not mentioned in description
  • You no longer need the product

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at refund@sigma.studio with:

  • Your order number
  • Email address used for purchase
  • Reason for refund request
  • Screenshots of any issues (if applicable)

Refund Process

Once we receive your refund request:

  • We will review your request within 2-3 business days
  • If approved, refund will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refund will be issued to your original payment method
  • You will receive a confirmation email

For Sellers

If you are a seller and a customer requests a refund for your product:

  • You will be notified of the refund request
  • Funds will be deducted from your balance
  • You may dispute the refund with evidence

Contact Us

For refund questions, contact: refund@sigma.studio
